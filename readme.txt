== Description ==
This plugin allows you to offer an extended service plan for wide variety of products you sell, including electronics, appliances, sports and fitness equipment, auto parts, furniture, jewellery and watches, musical instruments, and much more.   
 
##overview	
After, Inc. (www.afterinc.com), is the leader in post-sale customer experience technology, providing predictive analytics, data-driven marketing strategies, reporting, and program administration to some of the world’s top brands for the past 15 years. Our solutions help manufacturers, retailers and TPAs transform their warranty programs, driving customer satisfaction post-purchase, higher product reliability, deeper brand equity and additional revenue / profit opportunities.
The After plan was created to enable merchants of any size to offer product protection plans and accidental protection. The After plan covers a wide variety of products, including electronics, appliances large and small, sports and fitness equipment, auto parts, furniture, jewellery and watches, musical instruments, and much more. Basically, any product susceptible to mechanical or electrical failure or accidental damage from handling is eligible.

##Account Setup
Start selling the After plan today!

  1. Download and install the After Product Protection Plan plugin.
  2. Sign up for a QuickCover account at  [ https://signup.quickcover.me/join/afterinc]( https://signup.quickcover.me/join/afterinc)  to enable API services, map product categories  and obtain pricing.
  3. Sync your store product catalog to the QuickCover product categories to enable sales of the After Product Protection Plan.
  
  
For every plan you sell, you capture the revenue upfront and After, Inc. will bill you the merchant price weekly. Please refer to additional set-up content and full documentation [here](https://docs.quickcover.me/).
